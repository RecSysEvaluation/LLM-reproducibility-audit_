We provide two checklist links that outline the general and LLM-specific variables required to improve reproducibility. These interactive checklists were developed using Checky and contain more than 50 questions used to analyze 64 research articles published in top-ranked conferences over the past three years. The first checklist covers general reproducibility variables, while the second focuses on LLM-specific variables. Additionally, an Excel sheet containing all considered reproducibility variables, including both general and LLM-specific ones, can be found here.
